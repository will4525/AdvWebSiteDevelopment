Xochimilco
Appetizers
Chilaquiles

Crispy tortilla chips simmered in a rich red or green salsa, topped with crema, queso fresco, onions, and a fried egg

Tamal

A traditional Mexican dish made of masa (corn dough) stuffed with savory fillings like meat, cheese, or vegetables, wrapped in a corn husk and steamed to perfection.

Sope

A thick, round tortilla-like base topped with refried beans, meat, lettuce, cheese, crema, and salsa.

Entrees
Ceviche

Fresh, raw shrimp marinated in tangy lime juice, mixed with chopped onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and a touch of chili for a refreshing, zesty dish.

Pozole

A hearty Mexican soup made with hominy, tender pork or chicken, and seasoned with garlic, chili, and other spices.

Tacos el Pastor

Marinated pork cooked on a vertical spit (trumpet), shaved thin and served in soft corn tortillas with pineapple, onions, and cilantro. A savory, smoky, and slightly sweet taco that’s a street food favorite!

Tlayuda

A large, crispy Oaxacan flatbread topped with refried beans, cheese, lettuce, avocado, steak, and salsa. Sometime known as the Mexican pizza.

Birria

A rich and savory stew made with tender, slow-cooked beef and goat, simmered in a flavorful blend of dried chilies, spices, and herbs. Served with its delicious broth and garnished with onions, cilantro, and lime. Also availible as quesabirria

Huarache

A thick, oblong tortilla topped with refried beans, meat, lettuce, cheese, creama, and salsa. A flavorful Mexican dish resembling a sandal (the meaning of "huarache").

Desserts
Flan

A smooth, creamy caramel custard dessert with a delicate vanilla flavor, topped with a rich caramel sauce.

Tres Leches Cake

A spongy cake soaked in a mix of three milks—evaporated milk, condensed milk, and heavy cream—creating a moist, indulgent dessert.

Jericalla

A traditional Mexican custard dessert, made with milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla, and a hint of cinnamon. It has a creamy texture with a subtle caramelized top,
