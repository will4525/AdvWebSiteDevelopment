Deep Roots

Xochimilco is where it all began. The waters of this historic waterway gave birth to the great Aztec civilization. The ancient city of Tenochtitlán, which rivaled Venice in its day, was surrounded by a grand network of canals. Almost all have since been filled in, but Xochimilco remains. This is the source of inspiration for our restaurant, which continues the ancient traditions that these waterways fostered.

Authentic Ingridents

At Xochimilco, we honor our culinary heritage by using only the finest authentic ingredients sourced from Mexico’s rich lands. From heirloom corn and hand-harvested cacao to rare regional chilies, every ingredient tells a story of tradition and passion. Through our commitment to sustainability and local sourcing, we bring the true essence of Mexican flavors to life, ensuring every dish reflects the depth and history of our culture.

Traditional Methods

We preserve the heart of our cuisine by embracing traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. From hand-grinding nixtamalized corn for fresh tortillas to slow-roasting meats over open flames, every technique honors the authenticity of Mexican gastronomy. By using clay comales, stone molcajetes, and time-honored preparation styles, we ensure that each dish carries the depth of flavor and heritage that defines true Mexican cooking.
