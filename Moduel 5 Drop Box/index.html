Who We Are

Here at Xochimilco we celebrate Mexico’s rich culinary heritage with gourmet, regionally inspired cuisine. Using ancestral cooking techniques and the finest ingredients, we create an authentic, high-end dining experience like no other.

Our restaurant showcases Mexico’s diverse regional flavors and heritage with premium ingredients. From handmade blue corn tortillas to slow-cooked meats, we offer an authentic, refined dining experience that transports guests to Mexico’s finest culinary destinations.
